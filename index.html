About Liatulon!

Here is some information about Liatulon


Liatulon is a Realm-based Minecraft server!


❓ | What?

Liatulon is a Realm-based Minecraft server! In this server, you can join 4 different realms that consist of different properties. Like, Economy or Survival. This server has no pay to win aspects! Will you fight your way to victory? Or will you fight your way to the leaderboards? The choice is yours!


❓ | Why?

Liatulon, with all its aspects, can be much more enjoyable than traditional hub servers as there is a lot more to do instead of joining random servers and playing for 10 minutes. Bored? Just go fishing, hunting, fighting or so much more!


❓ | When?

Liatulon doesn't have a set release date but we're hoping it to release in the next few months! You will be pinged a day before server release.


Information on here is subject to change.
